"You Want To Do WHAT!?"

When I told my fiancé I wanted to learn computer programming she thought I was off my rocker. Her precise words were: “You want to do what!?” At age 32 and working in Hospitality for over 6 years with little computer background, I can understand why she thought I was crazy at first. I then began to explain that I was bored and fed up with Food and Beverage, and that I felt that our lives would be better if I focused on a career in Web Development.

I soon found out after reading an article about growth mindset that I was unhappy because I was not being challenged. I have always been the person that embraces change and takes on challenges with excitement. Learning to program has not been easy, but I am willing to put forth the effort. Programming pushes me out of my comfort zone on a daily basis. I love that if I don’t know the solution to a problem off the top of my head I can quickly learn how to solve the problem with a little research. Learning to program has truly made me a happier individual.
